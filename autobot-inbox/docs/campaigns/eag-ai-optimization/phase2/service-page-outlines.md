   - Typical projects: greenfield buildouts, retrofits of existing industrial space, scaling from small to commercial

   - Phase 1: Feasibility — square footage, state regulations, license type, budget range

   - CO2: supercritical vs subcritical, equipment footprint, typical applications
   - Solventless: rosin, ice water hash — different regulatory profile, facility requirements

4. Common Mistakes EAG Prevents
   - Undersizing HVAC for solvent load — the most expensive retrofit mistake
   - Buying equipment before designing the room
   - Ignoring state-specific extraction license requirements during facility design
   - Overlooking egress and emergency shutoff placement

### Page 4: Cannabis Operational Analysis & Auditing
**URL:** /services/operational-analysis
**Target words:** 600+

**H1:** Cannabis Operational Analysis & Facility Auditing

**H2 sections:**
1. What an Operational Audit Covers
   - Compliance: license conditions, state regulations, SOP adherence
   - Yield and throughput: extraction efficiency, waste rates, batch consistency
   - Cost structure: COGS breakdown, labor efficiency, utility costs
   - Staffing: skill gaps, org structure, training needs
   - Process flow: bottlenecks, redundant steps, capacity constraints

2. Who Gets an Operational Audit
   - Facilities that are underperforming vs. projections
   - Investors doing diligence on an acquisition target
   - Operators preparing for regulatory inspection
   - Owners who suspect internal inefficiency but can't pinpoint it

3. EAG's Audit Process
   - On-site assessment: 1-3 days depending on facility size
   - Documentation review: SOPs, batch records, financial reports
   - Interview process: leadership, supervisors, operators
   - Findings report: prioritized by impact and effort
   - Implementation roadmap: what to fix first and how

4. Typical ROI
   - Yield improvements of 5-15% in extraction operations
   - Labor cost reductions through workflow optimization
   - Avoided regulatory action through proactive compliance remediation

5. CTA: Schedule an Operational Audit
