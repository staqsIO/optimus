### Cannabis License Application Consulting

Winning a cannabis license is not about luck — it's about preparation, specificity, and demonstrating to regulators that you can operate a safe, compliant, and professionally-managed cannabis business. In competitive limited-license markets, the quality of your application is often the deciding factor between approval and rejection.

Elevated Advisory Group provides full-service cannabis license application support across the United States. We have helped clients secure cultivation, manufacturing, retail, and distribution licenses in multiple states, including competitive merit-based application processes where the difference between a winning and losing application comes down to technical depth and operational credibility.

### What's Included in a License Application Engagement

#### Regulatory Research & Strategy

Before writing a single word of your application, we analyze the specific regulatory framework for your license type in your state. This includes:

- License type eligibility and prerequisites
- Application scoring rubric (in merit-based states)
- Local approval requirements (zoning, conditional use permits, local authorization letters)
- Timeline: application window, review period, typical approval timeframe
- Competitive landscape: how many licenses are available, how many applicants are expected

#### Facility Planning Documentation

Most cannabis license applications require detailed facility plans as part of the application — not approximations. We produce:

- Dimensioned floor plans showing processing, storage, and security zones
- Security system specifications (cameras, access control, alarm systems)
- Waste disposal procedures
- Odor control plans
- Ventilation and HVAC specifications (especially for manufacturing)

These documents serve double duty: they satisfy application requirements and give you a construction roadmap once you're licensed.

#### Application Writing & Review

We draft or review narrative sections of your application, including:

- Business plan and operational narrative
- Security plan
- Environmental and waste management plan
- Social equity plan (in states with equity requirements)
- Community impact plan
- Key personnel qualifications and background summaries

Our writing is precise, specific, and responsive to the actual scoring criteria — not generic cannabis industry boilerplate.

#### SOPs for Application Purposes

Many state applications require submission of standard operating procedures at the application stage. We draft licensing-grade SOPs for:

- Inventory tracking and seed-to-sale compliance (METRC or state-specific system)
- Employee training programs
- Security procedures
- Product testing and quality control
- Record keeping

### State-Specific Considerations

Cannabis licensing is not uniform across states. A few critical variables that shape application strategy:

- **Merit-based vs. lottery systems** — In merit-based states (Illinois, New Jersey, New York), application quality is everything. In lottery states, compliance is the bar.
- **Social equity provisions** — Many states give preference or additional points to equity applicants. We help operators understand whether they qualify and how to document it.
- **Local approval requirements** — Some states require a local license or letter of support before the state application can be submitted.
- **Buildout requirements** — Some states require a lease or property control agreement at time of application; others allow conditional approval first.
