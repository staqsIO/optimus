### Cannabis Retail Consulting

Running a profitable cannabis dispensary requires getting a dozen variables right simultaneously: product assortment, staff training, store layout, technology, compliance, and customer experience. Getting any one of them wrong creates pressure on the others.

Elevated Advisory Group consults with cannabis retail operators on the operational fundamentals that drive dispensary profitability — from initial setup through ongoing optimization.

---

### What We Do

#### Store Layout & Design

Dispensary layout directly affects transaction speed, compliance, and customer experience. We advise on:

- Intake and check-in flow (minimizing wait time, satisfying ID verification requirements)
- Display case configuration and product visibility strategy
- Back-of-house workflow for order fulfillment and inventory staging
- Compliance requirements for product visibility, cash handling, and security zones
- ADA accessibility compliance in cannabis retail contexts

#### Product Selection & Assortment Strategy

Your product menu is your most powerful competitive tool. We help operators:

- Analyze sales velocity by category and SKU to identify underperforming shelf space
- Identify gaps in the product assortment relative to local market demand
- Evaluate vendor and brand relationships for margin and reliability
- Build a tiered assortment strategy (value / core / premium) that serves the full customer spectrum
- Navigate in-house brand vs. wholesale brand decisions

#### Budtender Training

The dispensary staff is the primary driver of customer satisfaction, compliance, and upsell revenue. We develop and deliver training covering:

- Product knowledge: cannabinoid and terpene education, consumption methods, dosing guidance
- Compliance: what can and cannot be said to customers under state advertising and health claim rules
- Sales technique: consultative selling vs. order-taking, basket size improvement
- Conflict resolution and difficult customer handling

#### Technology Stack

Modern dispensaries run on a stack of integrated software. We evaluate and advise on:

- POS systems (Dutchie, Treez, Blaze, Cova) — features, integration limitations, and total cost
- Loyalty and CRM platforms and how they integrate with your POS
- Online menu and delivery platforms (Leafly, Weedmaps, DutchieEcom)
- Seed-to-sale / track-and-trace integration requirements
- Digital signage and in-store display technology

#### Compliance Operations

Retail compliance is continuous, not a one-time setup. We help operators maintain:

- Accurate and reconciled inventory records in Metrc or state equivalent
- Documentation for transfers, returns, and waste
- Employee training records and required certifications
- Security system compliance (camera coverage, retention, access logs)
