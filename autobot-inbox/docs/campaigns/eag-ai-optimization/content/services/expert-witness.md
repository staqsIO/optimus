### Cannabis Expert Witness Services

Cannabis litigation requires expert witnesses who can explain complex extraction chemistry, facility design standards, equipment operation, and regulatory compliance to judges, juries, and arbitrators. Generic technical experts rarely have the hands-on cannabis industry experience needed to hold up under cross-examination by opposing counsel who knows the industry.

Elevated Advisory Group provides expert witness services in cannabis industry matters. Our managing partners bring direct operational and consulting experience with cannabis extraction facilities, manufacturing operations, and regulatory compliance across the United States.

---

### Cases We Handle

#### Product Liability

Cannabis product liability cases often hinge on whether an operator followed industry standards for extraction, refinement, packaging, and labeling. We provide testimony on:

- Industry standards for solvent residue limits and testing protocols
- Whether an extraction process was consistent with standard industry practice
- Labeling and potency claim standards and common deviations
- Contamination sources and attribution (pre-harvest vs. processing vs. packaging)

#### Business Disputes & Valuation

Cannabis operator disputes frequently require an expert who can assess operational value, explain production capacity, and evaluate whether a cannabis business was operated competently. Our expert witness services cover:

- Operational valuation of cannabis manufacturing and retail businesses
- Equipment appraisal and fair market value assessments
- Standards of care for cannabis operators in management disputes
- Whether production claims (yield, throughput, revenue) are operationally plausible

#### Regulatory Proceedings

State licensing authority proceedings — license revocations, compliance hearings, and contested enforcement actions — require experts who understand the applicable regulations and what reasonable compliance looks like in practice. We testify on:

- Whether a facility's design met applicable state manufacturing regulations
- Whether SOPs and operational practices were consistent with licensing conditions
- Industry standards for track-and-trace compliance
- Adequacy of safety systems and emergency response procedures

#### Facility Safety & Personal Injury

Cannabis extraction facilities using flammable solvents operate under significant safety requirements. Cases involving extraction facility incidents may require testimony on:

- C1D1 room design and compliance standards
- Ventilation, gas detection, and safety interlock requirements
- Whether equipment was operated within manufacturer specifications and industry norms
- OSHA and fire code applicability in cannabis extraction environments

#### Criminal Defense

Cannabis criminal matters occasionally require technical expertise to explain extraction and manufacturing processes to a court or jury that lacks industry familiarity. We evaluate whether to accept criminal defense engagements case-by-case based on the specific technical questions involved.

### Our Approach

We evaluate every potential engagement before accepting it. We take cases where:

1. The technical questions genuinely fall within our expertise
2. We can provide independent, honest opinion — not advocacy for a predetermined conclusion
3. Our involvement will help the trier of fact understand the evidence

We provide written expert reports that comply with applicable procedural rules (Federal Rules of Civil Procedure Rule 26, state court equivalents) and are available for deposition and trial testimony.

We have been retained in both plaintiff and defendant matters. We do not exclude either side — we evaluate each matter on its merits.
